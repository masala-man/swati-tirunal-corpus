$CHARANAM

aviḷambaṃ suśiśirēṇāmbhasā surabhiṇā
savinayādaramahō saṃsnāpayāmīha
bhuvanapāvanamūrttē bhuvi kimitō bhāgyaṃ!

$CHARANAM

samalaṅkarōmi bhūṣaṇairavi varairahaṃ
